Method & caveats

  • Truth source: EIA-923 monthly net generation back to each plant's COD (up to ~9 yrs) vs. an ERA5-driven physics model, in capacity-factor space.
  • Curtailment is included. EIA actuals embed dispatch curtailment (32–48% at some congested South-Texas nodes), so the factor is exact for revenue but a floor for pure weather bias.
  • Wind extremes (Anacacho 4.6×, El Algodon 4.0×) pair short records or heavy curtailment with the coastal jet — treat as screening, validate before contract use.
  • Solar tracking/tilt/DC:AC now read per-plant from EIA-860 Schedule 3.3; this sharpens individual plants though the fleet's ~12% offset is a uniform model bias.
  • Crosswalk from a 496-node table is unvalidated per-plant (it mis-mapped Stafford to a wind farm); a CF-sanity gate catches gross errors.
  • Coverage: 119 / 120 wind and 57 / 69 solar plants; the rest dropped for <24 months of history or transient fetch failures.
